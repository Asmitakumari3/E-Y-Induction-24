Loops
	This are used for iterations - repetation untill you want it to repeat

	Types of loops
		1. For loop (this is when you know how many repeatations you want to perform - 				eg - 5 times or 10 times)

		2. While loop (this is when you want to repeate the code until it meets the condition - eg ATM machine)

		3. Foreach loop - (special loop for Collections like Array and list)
